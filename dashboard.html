Business Insights
Insight 01 — Pricing
Pro tier churns at 71% — higher than Free — revealing a value gap at the $99/month price point.
SQL Q1: Pro churn rate is 71% vs 67% Free, 17% Starter, and 0% Enterprise. Pro churners average 1.8 support tickets each — 67% billing-related. Despite this, Pro average LTV is $990, meaning customers initially see value but lose confidence over time.
Action: Assign CSMs to Pro accounts logging fewer than 10 logins/month. Trigger proactive ROI calls at 60 and 90 days post-signup.
High Impact
Insight 02 — Onboarding
Customers completing all onboarding steps show near-zero 90-day churn; each step skipped triples dropout risk.
MongoDB Q3: Churned users completed 2.8 onboarding steps on average vs 5.4 for retained. Only 31% of churned customers reached "first feature used" vs 78% retained. All customers who completed billing setup had 0% churn within 90 days.
Action: Reduce onboarding to 4 key actions. Trigger email at 48 hours for users who stall after email verification.
High Impact
Insight 03 — Expansion
Enterprise generates 6.7× more LTV than Pro at 0% churn — converting high-usage Pro accounts is the top growth lever.
SQL Q2: Enterprise avg LTV is $6,648 vs $990 Pro. Enterprise accounts log 29 sessions/month with 89 feature interactions and 960 session minutes. Zero churn over 24 months. SQL Q5 cross-reference found 3 Pro accounts already at Enterprise engagement levels.
Action: Offer those 3 accounts a 30-day white-glove Enterprise trial with dedicated onboarding.
High Impact
