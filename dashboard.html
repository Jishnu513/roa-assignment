Business Insights
Insight 01 — Pricing
Pro tier churns at 71% — higher than Free — revealing a value gap at $99/month.
SQL Q1: Pro churn is 71% vs 67% Free and 17% Starter. Pro churners average 1.8 support tickets each — 67% billing-related. Enterprise shows 0% churn at $299/month, proving price is not the issue.
Action: Assign CSMs to Pro accounts with under 10 logins/month. Trigger ROI check-in calls at day 60 and 90.
HIGH IMPACT
Insight 02 — Onboarding
Customers completing all onboarding steps have near-zero 90-day churn; each skipped step triples dropout probability.
MongoDB Q3: churned users completed 2.8 steps vs 5.4 for retained. Only 31% of churned users reached "first feature used" vs 78% retained. Every customer who completed billing setup had 0% churn in 90 days.
Action: Reduce onboarding to 4 key actions. Send nudge email at 48h for users stalled after verification.
HIGH IMPACT
Insight 03 — Expansion
Enterprise generates 6.7× more LTV than Pro at 0% churn — upgrading high-usage Pro accounts is the top growth lever.
SQL Q2: Enterprise avg LTV $6,648 vs $990 Pro. Enterprise logs 29 sessions/month with 89 feature interactions and 960 session minutes. SQL Q5 identified 3 Pro accounts already at Enterprise engagement levels.
Action: Offer those 3 Pro accounts a 30-day white-glove Enterprise trial with dedicated onboarding.
HIGH IMPACT
